# StudioPulse — `SP-react` (Frontend)

A modern practice & assignment web app for private music studios. **StudioPulse** structures learning, reduces the overhead of assigning/checking homework, and makes progress crystal-clear for students and parents.

> This repo is the **frontend** (React). A companion backend project provides auth, data storage, and safe API proxying.

---

## Goals for the project StudioPulse:

1. **Structure the learning process**
2. **Minimize teacher time** for organizing homework & giving feedback
3. **Facilitate evaluation and assessment**
4. **Provide prompt, clear progress reports** to parents
5. **Increase exam success rates**
6. **Increase homework effectiveness**
7. **Increase awareness of progress**

This is the Final Project for the **TripleTen Software Engineering Bootcamp**.

---

The app will welcome the students on the **Homepage** with instructions how to navigate the app, and enthusiastic and motivational information about the "Theory of 10,000 hours" and will allow the students to sign up or sign in. When they log in they can see their profile page with:

- **Nav-Bar** with links to take them to edit their profile info, avatar, or direct them back to the homepage

- Under the Nav-Bar there will be an **outlined section with Reminders** (for instance: Your next class is scheduled for \_**\_, or Your assignment is due \_\_**), that can be closed by pushing the ‘x’ button on the top right of the section

- **Left Side-Bar** with links to: 1. **Current Homework assignment:** separate links for: Scales, Piece A, Piece B, Piece C, Sight Reading, Aural Training; 2. **Tools:** Calendar, Metronome, Timer; 3. **History:** Practice log, Repertoire, Performing history.

- **Body** (to the right of the SideBar) will contain **The Current Assignment Summary** with pushable links that will open a detailed description of the separate elements of the Summary (for example: Scales, Piece A, Piece B, Piece C, Sight Reading, Aural Training). This detailed description will have current and target tempos for each element, sound quality and dynamics assignment, notes on articulation and , may have links to YouTube instructional videos Students will be checking off tasks in the **task list** at the bottom of every detailed description section.

- The students will be able to leave comments for the teacher in the **chat**. Also they will be able to upload a picture or a short video of their homework.

- The **teacher’s version** of this app will be developed after the successful testing of the student's version.
